# 🌾 KrishiBOT
KrishiBOT is an AI-powered agricultural assistance system designed to support farmers by providing accessible, timely, and easy-to-understand information related to crop guidance, soil health awareness, market insights, and government scheme information. Farmers often face challenges such as fragmented information sources, language barriers, and limited access to reliable advisory services, which can directly impact decision-making and productivity. KrishiBOT addresses these challenges by offering a single, conversational platform that focuses on clarity, usability, and real-world relevance rather than technical complexity. The system leverages AI prompt engineering to understand user intent and generate context-aware responses that guide farmers through common agricultural concerns in a simple and farmer-friendly manner. Instead of overwhelming users with raw data, KrishiBOT emphasizes practical guidance and meaningful explanations that are easy to interpret and apply. Designed as a hackathon prototype, the project prioritizes user experience, accessibility, and impact, demonstrating how AI tools can be used effectively even without extensive model training or heavy infrastructure. The interface is intentionally kept minimal and intuitive, making it suitable for users with varying levels of digital literacy. KrishiBOT is built with scalability in mind and can be extended in the future to include multilingual support, real-time integrations such as weather updates and market prices, voice-based interaction, and deployment as a web or mobile application. Overall, KrishiBOT represents a socially driven AI solution that combines thoughtful design with intelligent technology to empower farmers, improve access to critical agricultural information, and highlight the potential of AI-driven systems in addressing real-world challenges.
